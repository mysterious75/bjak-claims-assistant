BJAK General Insurance FAQ - Policy and Claims

Q: How do I check my policy status?
A: Check policy status through: 1) BJAK App > My Policies, 2) Website > Login > Policy Dashboard, 3) Call customer care with policy number. Policy status shows active, expired, or lapsed.

Q: What is the claim settlement process?
A: Claim process: 1) Intimate claim (app/call), 2) Submit documents, 3) Survey/verification, 4) Claim approval, 5) Payment release. Average time: 7-15 days depending on claim type.

Q: How do I renew my expired policy?
A: For expired policy: 1) Login to BJAK app, 2) Go to expired policies, 3) Click Renew, 4) Pay premium, 5) Policy is reactivated. Note: Lapse超过90 days may require fresh medical checkup.

Q: What is the grace period for premium payment?
A: Grace period is 30 days from due date. During grace period: 1) Policy remains active, 2) Coverage continues, 3) No interest charged. After grace period, policy lapses.

Q: How do I update my contact details?
A: Update details through: 1) BJAK App > Profile > Edit, 2) Website > Account Settings, 3) Submit written request with ID proof. Changes take 24-48 hours to reflect.

Q: What is the process for policy endorsement?
A: For endorsements (name change, address change, nominee change): 1) Submit request with supporting documents, 2) Pay endorsement fee if applicable, 3) Updated policy issued within 7 days.

Q: How do I cancel my policy?
A: To cancel: 1) Submit written request, 2) Return original policy document, 3) Refund calculated as per short-period scales, 4) Refund credited within 15 business days.

Q: What is short-period cancellation refund?
A: Refund scales: 1) Within 1 month: 75% refund, 2) 1-3 months: 50% refund, 3) 3-6 months: 25% refund, 4) Beyond 6 months: No refund. Premium is calculated on pro-rata basis.

Q: How do I check claim status?
A: Check claim status: 1) BJAK App > Claims > Track, 2) Enter claim ID, 3) View timeline and current status. You can also call customer care with claim ID.

Q: What is the escalation process for delayed claims?
A: For delayed claims: 1) First escalate to claims manager, 2) If unresolved, file complaint with insurance ombudsman, 3) Contact IRDA for regulatory issues. Keep all correspondence records.
