BJAK Health Insurance - Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is covered under health insurance?
A: BJAK health insurance covers hospitalization, day care procedures, pre and post hospitalization expenses, ambulance charges, and domiciliary hospitalization. Coverage includes room charges, doctor fees, medicine costs, and diagnostic tests.

Q: What documents are required for health insurance claim?
A: You need to submit: 1) Claim form (duly filled), 2) Original hospital bills and receipts, 3) Discharge summary, 4) Policy number, 5) Medical reports and prescriptions, 6) Bank details for reimbursement.

Q: How long does claim processing take?
A: Standard claims are processed within 7-10 business days. Cashless claims at network hospitals are processed within 24 hours. Complex claims may take up to 30 days for verification.

Q: What is not covered under health insurance?
A: Exclusions include: pre-existing diseases (first 48 months), cosmetic treatments, self-inflicted injuries, substance abuse, dental treatment (unless due to accident), and expenses not related to medical treatment.

Q: How do I renew my health insurance policy?
A: You can renew through: 1) BJAK app - Go to Policies > Select Policy > Renew, 2) Website - Login and click Renew, 3) Call customer care at 1800-XXX-XXXX. Renew before due date to avoid policy lapse.

Q: What is cashless claim facility?
A: Cashless claim allows you to get treatment at network hospitals without paying upfront. BJAK settles the bill directly with the hospital. You only pay for non-covered expenses.

Q: How do I find network hospitals?
A: Use the BJAK app: Go to Network Hospitals > Enter your city > View list. You can also call customer care for the nearest network hospital.

Q: What is the claim process for day care procedures?
A: For day care procedures: 1) Intimate claim through app or call, 2) Get treatment at network hospital, 3) Submit documents within 15 days, 4) Claim is processed within 7 days.

Q: Can I claim for pre and post hospitalization expenses?
A: Yes, BJAK covers pre-hospitalization expenses (30 days before admission) and post-hospitalization expenses (60 days after discharge) related to the treated condition.

Q: What is the waiting period for claims?
A: Waiting periods: 1) Initial waiting: 30 days from policy start, 2) Pre-existing diseases: 48 months, 3) Specific diseases: 24 months. Accidents have no waiting period.
